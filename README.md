## About Me

I am a researcher and academic specializing in the intersection of Federated Learning, Quantum Machine Learning, and Cybersecurity. My work is driven by the goal of empowering Industrial IoT and Edge Computing through secure, distributed, and quantum-enhanced AI systems.
